How it all started

Every profesional started as a student. As the founders of Peersview, we realized that what students need most to become succesful is a mix of academic support and a sense of community.

To achieve this, we designed Peersview to increase peer-to-peer interactions among students, with an innovative blend of social networking features.

Here at Peersview, we foster an incredibly diverse community of students; it is accessible to users from all over the world. Our vision is to have every student on our platform feel empowered and connected from debating about topics in college to discussing professional passions. Ultimately, the goal of Peersview is to connect students with similar interests, likes and opinions in order to help them grow into successful professionals.

We simultaneously provide the best tools for obtaining knowledge as well as the ability to create new relationships in a way that has proven to be enjoyable to students.
