ACADEMICS & INSTRUCTORS

Transform your classroom into an online community of learners. Take advantage of our simple tools designed to help you create engaging learning experiences.

add

Student Assessment tool & Peer Review

  • Instructors can encourage students to test each other with formal questions as a simple way to motivate learning and collaboration between learners.
  • Instructors can also participate by adding some assignment questions for their students. You can use this to track your student’s learning in real-time.
  • We advise instructors to either split the topics covered within the course between students or distribute it in groups. These courses should inspire intelligent discussion, allowing students to pose questions about their assigned topics.
  • We recommend that instructors allocate a small percentage of course marks for participation. This motivates students to contribute quality answers to the questions posted.
add

Identify gaps in Learning Outcomes

  • Instructors can use students’ answers to identify and address common misunderstanding. It can be used to reveal further insight into the students’ perception of topics covered.
  • Instructors can get a sense of which topics students are more confident with and which topics students are not engaged with.

Student interaction is critical for academic success. The Peersview campus encourages dynamic online discussions between students.

How it works

Students & Instructors post questions on various topics.
Students rate each question according to its difficulty and answer the questions.
Instructors are updated with the questions student rate as the most difficult.
Instructors provide feedback to the difficult questions.
